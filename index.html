Chenab Bridge

Where Steel Meets the Sky

The Beginning of a Dream

Built under the USBRL project to connect Kashmir to India by rail, this audacious dream faced extreme terrain and natural challenges. Located between Bakkal and Kauri in the Reasi district, it spans a gorge 359m deep.

World Record Specifications

Feature Details
Length1,315 meters
Main Arch Span467 meters
Height above Chenab359 meters (higher than Eiffel Tower)
Steel Used28,000 tonnes
Wind ResistanceUp to 266 km/h
Seismic ZoneZone IV
Completion2022 (Testing in 2023)

Engineering Marvel

  • Blast-resistant design using special steel
  • Wind tunnel tested in Denmark & Germany
  • Seismic & sensor-based monitoring system
  • Longest cable crane system across a gorge (915m)
  • First Indian railway bridge to use such advanced tech

Symbolism

  • Reduces travel time to Kashmir Valley
  • Boosts local economy and tourism
  • Strengthens troop logistics and national security
  • Symbol of unity and engineering excellence
  • Trial run completed on 13 August 2023
